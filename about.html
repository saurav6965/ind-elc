About Indian Electricity

Indian Electricity, is a CSE326 Internet programming class group project, is an static Online Electric Bill Payment, Services & Retail Electricity Distribution site. It serves over three million consumers spread across 400 sq. kms in Punjab and its suburbs with 99.99% reliability, which is among the highest in the country. Indian Electricity meets close to 2,000 MW of power demand in India's largest and the most efficient power distribution network.

As an organization, Indian Electricity believes in the motto - The Power of Service. It is born of the will to make a difference and change things for the better, so that everyone can power their dreams and live a stress-free life

Indian Electricity continues the quest of providing the best quality service to the customers entrusted into the brand motto of the Power of Service. The company is committed to creating new & innovative approaches in operations and services that contribute to the development of the customers.
